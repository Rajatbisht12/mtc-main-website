Cognition : A DS CP PAATHSHALA

Dated : 6 June 2022- 31 July 2022

In Early June, Microsoft Technical Community inaugurated the beginning of a two-month journey of “Cognition: A DS CP Paathshala” to train the brains of the aspiring tech enthusiasts by offering a plethora of opportunities and a marathon of questions and quest to help them explore the looping world of data structures and competitive programming. The paathshala aimed on bringing all computer science undergrads and enthusiasts under one roof and help them ride their journey from being a beginner to an expert of the looping concepts of data structures. MTC, as an organization, was determined to highlight the importance of data structures in the field of computer science as we believe that in our field data dominates. Data structures are central to programming and if one aspires to be in the centre of expertise in this field, he needs to hold a strong grip on the dangling concepts of data structures. The Event that took flight on June 6 was scheduled to take place in two prominent programming language of the tech industry: Java and C++. Participants had the liberty to choose any one of these two languages and build their CV to be a step ahead of the crowd.
The itinerary followed for the Paathshala was:

The Dangling Concepts of C++ made easy:

Days of Conduction: Conducted on Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
Time: 7:00 -8:30 p.m

The Looping World of Java:

Conducted on Monday, Wednesday, Friday
Time: 7:00 -8:30 p.m
The 55 days event that got a triumphant conclusion on 31st July 2022 was, to quote in words, an immense success that drives us with the determination to organize more such events targeting the different domains and aspects of Computer Science and Technology in the near future. The Dangling Concepts of C++ were made easy under the mentorship of Mr Aditya Kumar, Mr Yash Raj Singh and Mr Vibhor Minocha while Mr. Sanidhya Soni took the lead in explaining the looping world of Java. To acknowledge the stead-fast dedication and determination, a digital badge of participation was awarded to all tech enthusiasts who met the criteria of 75% attendance for the paathshala. MTC, as an organization, believes that knowledge opens doors for opportunities, opportunities open doors for achievement, achievement is success. We promise to help you build your knowledge that’ll lead you to a plethora of opportunities and wish to see you climb the ladder of success. Therefore, keep on buckle down and prepare yourself to be a part of many more tech events to build up a new adventure that leads you to the ultimate success.
