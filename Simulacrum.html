SIMULACRUM

In a dazzling display of creativity and camaraderie, the Microsoft Technical Community orchestrated the extraordinary SIMULACRUM event! It encompassed three domains of boundless learning and artistic expression. About the event: The first domain featured an engaging workshop on Photoshop, Premier Pro, and color theory. This virtual workshop took place from the 29th to the 31st of October 2023, running from 7 to 9 pm each day. What's more, it was offered completely free of cost to all members, and a certificate of participation was provided to commemorate the event. This event, along with the photo gallery, was accessible to photography enthusiasts across India. On the 30th of October, the second domain led the participants on an enjoyable photo walk from 11 am to 12:30 pm and it, too, was provided at no charge. The culmination was the third domain - a captivating photo gallery showcasing various themes including portrait, landscape, wildlife, monochrome, still-life, and wisdom. This event was truly a testament to the creativity and skills of the participants. The event was a great success, and the positive response from our members has been truly heartening. We look forward to adding even more stars to our Microsoft Technical Community in the future. Thank you all for making this event such a memorable one!
